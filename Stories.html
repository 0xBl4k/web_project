Story 2 :Sarah's Cybersecurity Success

Sarah started in IT support but was inspired to shift to cybersecurity after a major data breach. She self-taught through courses and certifications. When her company faced a ransomware attack, Sarah acted fast, preventing major damage. Her skills earned her a promotion, and she later became a cybersecurity expert at a leading firm, all thanks to her dedication and quick thinking.
